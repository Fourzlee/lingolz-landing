Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Lingolz is a product of Mach6 GTM LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Lingolz.

Information We Collect

When you use Lingolz through Telegram, WhatsApp, or other messaging platforms, we receive:

  • Your messaging platform user ID (for example, your Telegram user ID or WhatsApp phone number where required by the platform)
  • The messages you send to Lingolz, including text you want translated or rephrased
  • Basic usage data such as the number of requests you have made and timestamps
  • Payment information processed by our payment provider if you upgrade to paid access (we do not store full card details)

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, or device information unless you provide them to us directly.

Platform Dependencies

Lingolz operates through third-party messaging platforms including Telegram and WhatsApp. These platforms have access to your messages and are subject to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any platform you use to access Lingolz.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide translation and reply suggestions in response to your messages
  • Track free tier usage limits and process payments for paid access
  • Improve the quality of our suggestions over time
  • Detect and prevent abuse or misuse of the service
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

Automated Processing

Lingolz uses artificial intelligence and large language models to generate translation and reply suggestions. These responses are automated and may not always be accurate. Lingolz does not make legal, financial, or other consequential decisions about users. You can request human review of any service decision by contacting us.

Legal Bases for Processing (EU and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: to improve our service, prevent abuse, and maintain security
  • Consent: where you have explicitly agreed to specific processing
  • Legal obligation: where required by law

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

How We Share Your Information

We send the text of your messages to third-party language model providers (currently Anthropic and OpenAI) to generate replies. These providers process the text under their own privacy terms and do not retain it for training purposes under our API agreements.

We use Stripe to process payments. Stripe receives payment information directly and processes it under its own privacy policy.

We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with advertisers.

We may share information when required by law, to protect our rights, to investigate fraud or abuse, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our service is operated. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms where required.

Data Retention

We retain message logs for up to 90 days for quality and abuse prevention purposes. Usage counts and payment records are retained as long as your account is active and as required for tax and accounting purposes (typically seven years for financial records). Aggregated, non-identifying data may be retained longer for analytics.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (for EU and UK users, this is your local data protection authority)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lingolzapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking

The Lingolz website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. The Lingolz messaging bot does not use cookies. If we add any tracking in the future, we will update this policy and obtain consent where required.

Children

Lingolz is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect information from children under these ages. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it. Parents who believe their child has provided information to us can contact us for deletion.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and access controls. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted at lingolz.com and the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
